Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply for a course?

To apply for a course, you can either fill out the contact form on this page, call our admissions office, or visit our campus in person. Our admissions team will guide you through the application process and requirements.

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary depending on the course. Generally, we require a high school diploma or equivalent for basic courses, and specific academic backgrounds for advanced courses. Please contact our admissions office for specific requirements for your course of interest.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, Hippocampus Academy offers merit-based scholarships for outstanding students. We also have special scholarship programs for financially disadvantaged students who demonstrate exceptional potential. Please inquire with our admissions team for more information.

Do you offer online courses?

Yes, we offer select courses in a hybrid format with both online and in-person components. Some introductory courses are available fully online. However, most of our specialized clinical courses require in-person attendance for practical sessions.
