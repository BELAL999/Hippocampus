About Hippocampus Academy

Excellence in medical education for Saudi students

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Hippocampus Academy was established with a clear mission: to provide Saudi students with world-class medical and dental education that meets international standards while being tailored to the unique healthcare needs of the Kingdom.

Named after the hippocampus—the part of the brain essential for learning and memory—our academy symbolizes our commitment to fostering deep, lasting knowledge that transforms students into skilled healthcare professionals.

What began as a small initiative with a handful of courses has grown into a comprehensive educational institution offering diverse programs across medicine and dentistry, all taught by leading experts in their fields.

Our Mission

To empower Saudi medical and dental students with exceptional education, practical skills, and ethical foundations that prepare them to excel in their healthcare careers and contribute to the advancement of healthcare in Saudi Arabia.

Our Vision

To be the leading medical and dental educational institution in Saudi Arabia, recognized for excellence in teaching, innovation in curriculum development, and producing graduates who become leaders in healthcare.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Maintaining the highest standards in education
  • Innovation: Embracing cutting-edge teaching methods and technologies
  • Ethics: Instilling strong ethical principles in healthcare practice
  • Inclusivity: Creating a welcoming environment for all students
  • Community: Building strong relationships with the healthcare community
