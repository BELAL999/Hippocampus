Frequently Asked Questions

What are the payment options?

We offer various payment options including credit card, bank transfer, and installment plans. After your application is accepted, our finance department will contact you with detailed instructions based on your preferred payment method.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we have a refund policy. If you withdraw at least 14 days before the course starts, you will receive a full refund minus an administrative fee. For withdrawals less than 14 days before the start date, a partial refund may be available. No refunds are provided once the course has begun.

What materials do I need for the course?

All required textbooks and digital resources are included in the course fee. You will need to have access to a computer with internet connection for accessing online materials. For specific courses, additional requirements will be communicated after enrollment.

How will I receive my certificate?

Upon successful completion of the course and passing all assessments, you will receive your certificate digitally within 2 weeks. Physical certificates can be mailed upon request for an additional fee.
